About Me

A technology mind growing into a business leader.

I started my journey as a Computer Science Engineering student, drawn to the logic, structure, and creativity behind software. That foundation led me to CME Group, where I spent two years as a software engineer and learned what it means to build for systems where reliability, scale, and precision matter.

Over time, I became just as curious about the decisions behind the product as the code behind the screen. That curiosity brought me to XIM Bhubaneswar, where I am pursuing MBA-BM and learning to think more deeply about strategy, leadership, markets, and people.

Healthcare is where my long-term ambition feels most alive. I want to understand how strong operations, thoughtful technology, and compassionate leadership can come together to create healthcare institutions that are efficient, trusted, and human.

Technology taught me to simplify complex problems, build with discipline, and respect the invisible details that make systems dependable.
